Binary Digits (Bits)

Brief History

Computing Applications

Eight bits make up a byte. Bytes are an important unit of memory. They are used to represent binary numbers. Binary numbers, in short, are made up of ones and zeros to represent larger numbers. The usage of ones and zeros may also represent on or off and true or false.
